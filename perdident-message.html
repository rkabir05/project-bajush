    PERSIDENT MESSAGE

    Jewellery is one of the oldest industries in the subcontinent. Gold ornaments have been used in this country since the time of kings and nawabs. The history of gold industry of this country is very glorious. The handicrafts made by the country's skilled goldsmiths are well-known everywhere. Beyond the borders of the country, it has gained reputation in different countries of the world.

    In order to survive in this competitive age of globalization and free trade, the door to export trade must be opened. The development of an industry occurs only when that industry spreads beyond the borders of its own country. Bangladesh Jeweller’s Association will play a leading role in the introduction and export of jewellery made in this country. In this case, the preferential trade facility or GSP facility in European countries will play a big role in the export of gold ornaments made in this country.

    Integrated and active organizational activities are the lifeblood of any organization. With this in mind, Bajus is playing a significant role in trying to strengthen the organizational process across the country by inculcating unity, trust and discipline among all in order to uphold its heritage.

    Bangladesh Jeweller’s Association will make a significant contribution to the future economy of this country. I look forward to your sincere co-operation in establishing a prosperous Bangladesh.

    Sayem Sobhan Anvir
    President
